Program Overview

From occasional Claude users
to systematic operators.

Welspun One already has Claude deployed company-wide. This training does not start from zero. It picks up where access ends and capability begins. Every module is built around real business scenarios from logistics, supply chain, analytics, and corporate functions.

Most employees with Claude access are using 20% of what it can do. This workshop closes that gap. Not with theory. With workflows they can run on Monday morning.

Who This Is For

Employees across Operations, Strategy, Business Development, Supply Chain, Analytics, and Corporate Functions. Participants already have Claude access and are not complete beginners.

What This Is Not

This is not an AI awareness session. No slide decks on what AI is. No time spent on fundamentals. Every minute is practical, demonstrated, and immediately applicable.

Primary Focus

Claude for Excel and Claude for workplace productivity. Participants leave with tested workflows, not notes to review later.

Delivery Method

Live demonstration-first. Every concept is shown before it is explained. Hands-on exercises follow each module. Participants build during the session, not after.

Curriculum

Six Modules. One Direction.

Each module builds on the last. From delegation mindset to prompt engineering, Excel workflows, document production, department-specific use cases, and finally persistent systems that run without you.

01

Claude at Work. How to Stop Prompting and Start Delegating

Shift participants from basic Q&A usage to systematic delegation of knowledge work tasks using Claude.

Topics Covered
  • Why most Claude usage is stuck at 20% of its actual capability
  • The delegation mindset. Treating Claude as an AI colleague, not a search engine
  • Context loading. How to give Claude everything it needs upfront
  • The difference between a prompt and a brief
  • Reading Claude's output critically. When to accept, when to push back
Outcome

Participants can write structured task briefs that produce finished, usable outputs from Claude in one or two turns.

Tools Covered
Claude.ai Claude Projects
02

Prompt Engineering for Corporate Teams

Equip participants with a repeatable prompting system for business scenarios across functions.

Topics Covered
  • The four components of a high-performance prompt. Role, context, task, output format
  • Constraint prompting. Telling Claude what NOT to do is as important as what to do
  • Iterative prompting. How to refine outputs in three turns instead of starting over
  • Prompt templates for recurring tasks. Reports, summaries, emails, analysis briefs
  • Department-specific prompt examples. Operations, Supply Chain, Strategy, Analytics
Outcome

Participants have a personal library of tested prompt templates they can use immediately for their function.

Tools Covered
Claude.ai
03

Claude for Excel and Data Work

Enable participants to use Claude as an active analysis partner for spreadsheet and data tasks, not just a formula generator.

Topics Covered
  • Formula generation and debugging. Describe what you want in plain English, get working formulas back
  • Data cleaning and restructuring. Inconsistent entries, duplicate logic, column transformations
  • Analysis requests from raw data. Trends, anomalies, summaries, comparisons across sheets
  • Building Excel reports using Claude. Structuring outputs, writing commentary, flagging key numbers
  • Claude for large datasets. Python-assisted chunking for files beyond standard paste limits
Outcome

Participants can offload formula writing, data cleaning, and report commentary to Claude and get accurate, usable outputs.

Tools Covered
Claude.ai Microsoft Excel Claude for Excel
04

Business Document and Report Production

Replace manual drafting of standard business documents with Claude-assisted production workflows.

Topics Covered
  • SOPs and process documentation. Dictate a process, Claude structures it into a formatted document
  • Business reports and summaries. Upload raw data or notes, get a structured report back
  • Email and communication drafting. Context-aware drafts that match corporate tone
  • Presentation structuring. Outline to slide narrative in one brief
  • Research synthesis. Upload multiple sources, get a consolidated briefing document
Outcome

Participants produce first drafts of reports, SOPs, emails, and presentations in a fraction of the current time.

Tools Covered
Claude.ai Google Drive Microsoft Word PowerPoint
05

Claude for Department-Specific Workflows

Translate general Claude capabilities into concrete, role-relevant workflows for every function in the room.

Topics Covered
  • Operations and Supply Chain. Vendor communication drafts, procurement summaries, delay analysis from data
  • Strategy and Business Development. Market research synthesis, competitive landscape briefs, deck narrative drafts
  • Analytics. Hypothesis framing, data commentary, insight summarization from outputs
  • Corporate Functions (HR, Finance, Admin). Policy drafts, budget commentary, meeting summary workflows
  • Live group exercise. Each department group maps one recurring weekly task to a Claude workflow
Outcome

Every participant leaves with at least one mapped Claude workflow specific to their function and measurable time savings identified.

Tools Covered
Claude.ai Claude Projects Google Sheets Excel
06

Memory, Projects, and Building Repeatable Systems

Move participants from one-off Claude interactions to persistent, structured workflows that build institutional value over time.

Topics Covered
  • Claude Projects. What they are, how to set them up, and why every team should have one per function
  • Project instructions. Writing the brief that makes every future interaction consistent
  • Uploading context files. Feeding Claude your SOPs, style guides, and data references
  • Building a team Claude workflow. One shared Project, consistent outputs, no single-person dependency
  • Skills overview. What they are, when to build one, and how to hand off to a non-technical team member
Outcome

Participants set up one functioning Claude Project for their team before the session ends, with instructions and at least one uploaded context file.

Tools Covered
Claude.ai Claude Projects Claude Skills
